Welcome to Swagbets,

a platform for speculating on future events with market-based odds.


Tokens are traded on various outcomes, with prices serving to aggregate relevant knowledge and insights distributed among many traders with disparate information and experience, allowing the best possible predictions to emerge regarding uncertain future events.

SwagBets is an example of a prediction market. Prediction markets serve as interesting demonstrations of market price discovery, and are often to said to outperform polls and pundits in predictive accuracy.






Take a look at the list of currently traded propositions. Select a proposition and outcome to view current odds and place bids.





View your funds, current stakes, payouts and pending bids. Tokens are paid out if and when your bet is deemed correct.





Open a new account to start trading. An account is required to place bids on tokens for any outcome, but not to view latest odds.
